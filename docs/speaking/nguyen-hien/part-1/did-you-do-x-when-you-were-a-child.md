## Synonyms

- Were a child
- Were little
- Were young
- Were small
- X years old
- Primary school
- "I can't properly remember exactly how old I was, but I was probably about x years old"
## Strategy
- Yes + reason (yes, when I was a child blah blah)
- No + reason

## Example

Did you enjoy singing when you were younger?
Yes, absolutely. My mom told me that when I was three years old I sank a lot. She even recorded me singing, and we still have the tape at home

Did you enjoy drawing lessons when you were a child?
No, not really I wasn't interested in drawing. I found it to be quite boring. I remembered to sleep during the art lessons and got quite low marks in that subject.


Did you often listen to music when you were a child?
Yes, I loved listening to music back in the days. I often tuned into local stations and they would stream some traditional pieces of music. I even brought the radio into bathroom to continue listening while showering.


Did you do household work when you were a child?
Yes, I was made to do some house chores when I was young. I was assigned to simple tasks like doing dishes or sweeping the floor. Those were ways of my parents to teach me about responsibility and life skills.

Did you see your grandparents often when you were a child?
Yes, that was it case back in the day. When I was young, my parents worked so hard that they didn't have time to look after me so my grandma and grandpa were my babysitter. They would take turn feeding me and taking me to places around the neighborhood.

Did you enjoy solving puzzles in your childhood?
No, not actually back in the days I was in the smartest kid in the group and any kind of person. Also a riddles would take me a significant amount of time to solve it. I was more of an outdoor kid I would love to play soccer hide and seek with my friends so yeah, I'm not that smart too. Finish a Rubiks cube for example.
