> Is it easy to learn how to swim?

Actually, it is very difficult, especially for those who are afraid of water - like me. The hardest part, I think, is to breath underwater. I spent over two months just to know the basic of it.

### It depends 

> Is it easy traveling around the city?

Well, it used to be a breeze commuting from one end of the city to another, but today - not so much. There are too many personal vehicles nowadays, and no one uses public means of transport. Because of those, traffic jam happens more freq, especially dur rush hour.

> Is it easy learning a new musical instrument

Well, it is always hard to try something new. Learning a musical instrument is not an exception. Take guitar for example. Playing it at the beginning is challenging - your finger can hurt a lot. But after a while, it starts to get easier as you get used to it.
