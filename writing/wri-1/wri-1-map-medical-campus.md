The diagrams give information about the current layout of the biomedical campus at an academic institution, along with the proposed plan of change in the future.

Overall, this site is expected to undergo substantial changes, being more densely packed with facilities. Key features of the plan include more specialized buildings being built and expanded, and the reduction of green space.

At the moment, there is only one car park positioned on the top right of the map, accessible through a nearby road. In the future, this will be for students only, as that for staff will be on the triangular plot on the bottom left. This site will be separated from the campus by a short path. The road system will be moved to the left and bottom borders, as through the existing walkway people will be able to access the center area.

More space will be utilized for the university's facilities. The former grassed area on the upper half will house new buildings for genetics (named "Genetic 1"), and bioimaging. On the bottom left, while the lab will remain unchanged, the biochemistry complex will have a new part stemming out from the current one. Lastly, the remaining quadrant on the lower right will see the biotech department double in size, yet the current one for genetics (renamed to "Generic 2") and lab will stay the same.
