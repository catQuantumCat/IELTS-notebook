Green spaces replacement with shared areas; major changes in internal spacing

The diagrams show the current office layout, along with the proposed design for future changes.

Overall, the office will undergo substantial transformation, specifically the replacement of green areas with shared spaces, and major displacements in the internal spacing. Key changes including the reposition of the main entrance, the repurposing of outdoor areas, and the expansion of office rooms.

One significant change involves the entrance and internal arrangement. At the present, the entrance is located on the right-hand side of the building, with four small offices arranged along the lower section. In the proposed plan, the entrance will be moved to the center position, while those office room will be merged into 2 larger areas, located on either side of the building. At the top of the building, the kitchen and canteen will be removed to make room for the newly expanded offices, while spacing for storage will remain unchanged.

In terms of the outdoor layout, both grassed area will be repurposed. The left lawn will be transformed into an outdoor space equipped with chairs, while the right green space will be redeveloped to be an extension of the indoor space. This new section will host the coffee machine near the storeroom, the relocated restrooms and the newly open meeting area in the lower corner of the layout.
