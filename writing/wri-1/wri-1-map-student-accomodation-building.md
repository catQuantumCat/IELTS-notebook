The maps give information about the layout changes applied to a student dormitory.

Overall, this structure has undergone significant transformations, with the intention to provide more dedicated spacing for students. This involved the outside area being greatly reduced to make room for parking and more internal spacing.

In 2010, there were two gardens outside the building, positioned north and south of it, with a single southwest main entrance. Now, the south green area has been completely replaced by a car park. Meanwhile, a new room filled a part of the opposite garden, which also provided another entry point.

Inside the building, there were major differences that took place here. In particular, with the removal of the livingroom and the extra spacing on the top side come two new student bedrooms - bringing the count of these areas from three to five. While the one near the bathroom remained unchanged, that of the bottom-right corner now features additional en-suite room. Besides being the place for cooking, the kitchen between two sleeping rooms now also hosts shared activities among residents.
