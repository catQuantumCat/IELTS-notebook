This diagram illustrates the process of collecting and filtering water, powered by solar energy and gravity.

Overall, there are several steps in this process, beginning with the capture of sunlight, and ending with the collection of drinking water in containers.

Initially, energy from the Sun is collected using solar panels, before being transformed into electricity by a converter. This is then utilized to power a pump, which is placed on top of the well and transports water for the next steps.

At the next stage, unfiltered water is pumped from the well to a tank, stationed on a high wooden platform. After which, it runs through a water filter system inside the tank to make it consumable. A tap is installed on the tube connecting this tank and a movable container (on the ground) - preventing liquid wastage. To collect it, one must open the tap, and gravity will fill the container with drinkable water, from a high elevation level to a lower one.
