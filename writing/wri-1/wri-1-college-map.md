


The maps give information about a college's current layout, and the proposed changes for the future.

Overall, **this site is predicted to undergo substantial transformations**, specifically the expansion of the indoor space, and the repurposing of outdoor features to be more car-centric. Key changes involve the broadening of parking, and the reorganisation and addition of rooms.

At the moment, three classrooms are located on either side of the internal section, with the reception on the right hand-side. In the proposed plan, the latter area will be moved to a center position, while two new classrooms will be added, with four of them on the right edge. On the left section, the library will be repositioned from the opposite back corner to be closer to the entrance, with a classroom and a lunch area next to it. Restroom facilities will remain on the back of the building, with a new shopping area left of it.

Regarding external space, changes will be made to prioritise automobiles. The north garden and footpaths surrounding the building will be removed to make room for the mentioned indoor extension. That will also introduce a new car park to the current western cluster, bringing the total count to three. Access to the building will no longer face away from the southern road, but will be across it - near the existing bus stop.
