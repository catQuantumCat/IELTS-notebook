## Why?
- The convenience of single-use plastic in today's household
	- Meat and vegetable when arrived home from supermarket are often wrapped in layers of plastic wrap and a plastic bag for the sake of convenience and safety. After unwrapped and have the inner content is cooked, the life cycle of these containers are far to reach the end.
	- This, multiply by the increasing number of residents thanks to the rise in population, means that there is an enormous amount of single-use, hard to dispose trash from families
- Moreover, the promotion of fast food chains, the rise of online order system and promotions from these to attract consumers, means that people often purchase ready-to-eat food. Beside the same packaging problem as stated, these food has much shorter shelf-life. As people often buy them in bulk to take the advantage of promotion -> more food gone bad -> more household waste.
-  Which could take a toll on the waste management system.

## How to stop?

- Actions from governing bodies to promote sustainable packaging materials
	- Either biodegradable, or one that can be reused many time
	- Example: Put higher tax on the production of plastic containers, while doing the opposite for paper or multi-purpose
- Raise awareness
	- Educational campaign
	- To understand consequence -> make concious choice
