>In some countries there is not enough recycling of waste materials (eg paper, glass and cans). What are the reasons and solutions?

- Reasons
	- People awareness + lack of enthusiasm
		- Fast pace of living -> no time allocated for recycling process + convenience of single-use products.
	- In developing countries -> lack of developed trash classification -> the efforts of the few people who cares are pointless.
- Solutions
	- On a lower level: There should be stimulus for those who do recycling on a daily basis
		- Example: In German, there are bottle collecting machines. For a certain amounts of bottle, citizens can earn a coupon as a payback for returning the bottle. which can be redeemed at any grocery store nation-wise
	- On a higher level: Investments on better waste processing pipeline -> making recycling easier and more convenient + special policies that focus on making recycled products more accessible 
		- (banning single-use plastic)
