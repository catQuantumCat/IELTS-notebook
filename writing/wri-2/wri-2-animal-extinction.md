The animal species are becoming extinct due to human activities on land and in sea. What are the reasons and solutions?

### Why: No reproduction -> die

- The natural habitat of these animals are disappearing at an alarming rate, due to human activities
- This is the main driver
	- -> Reduction in breeding capacity -> population decline -> extinction
- Illegal hunting and trafficking
	- Poaching wild animals for tigers for their skins and bones -> lead to the death of these animals -> lower population + no breeding -> extinction
- Climate change:
	- Significant changes in climate -> make current habitat not suitable, or animals cannot adapt quick enough. They are forced to migrate to less ideal environment, either facing the challenge of being prey to other animals, or die
### Concequence
- Lost in biodiversity + significant alter in food chain. This could lead to the deaths of other animals, and disappearance of different ecological communities
- Some are beneficial in human's food chain, disease prevention, and pharmarceutical production
### Solution
- Governmental bodies + Eco organization recognise areas to protect, and put effort in recovering and maintaining
- Governmentail bodies should make wild animal trading illegal -> also raise awareness.
