>People think that government should increase the cost of fuel for cars and other vehicles to solve environmental problems. Give your opinion.

It's a common point of view that petrol for various transportations should be made more expensive by regulators to deal with environmental issues. From my perspective, while there are some certain benefits to this approach — it's not the ultimate solution, and the combination of some long-term strategies might together have a greater impact.
