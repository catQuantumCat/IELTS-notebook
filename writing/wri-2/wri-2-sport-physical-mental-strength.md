>Some people think that physical strength is important for success in sport, while other people think that mental strength is more important. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Whether physical or mental power is the ultimate key to success in sport is still up for debate, with convincing arguments from both sides. From my perspective, while a positive mental state gives athletes an undeniable advantage — it's the physical strength that empowers winning sportsmen.
