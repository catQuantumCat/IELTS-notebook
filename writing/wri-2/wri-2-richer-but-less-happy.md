What are the causes?


	Amid the current time period with unprecedented material wealth and easy access to medical and fitness services, it is harder for the younger generation to be satisfied for what they have achieved. We are fortunate to live in an era with relative political stability and technical advancements. But as those benefits are shared to everyone, it takes a lot more for these people to stand out, to climb the career ladder, and eventually earn a stable life. Those who can not keep up with this pace of life are faced with pressures of not meeting the societal expectations. This is why many office workers nowadays are willing to work after hours, staying inside their cubicle till late. Despite a higher pay and recognition from their colleagues and managers, less time in a day is dedicated for their personal life. The young people nowadays do earn more, have a longer lifespan, and live a healthier life - but at the cost of their happiness.
