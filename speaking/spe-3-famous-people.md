1.**Who should people ask for advice on big issues, family members or friends?**
- Depends on what kind of problem you're seeking advice for; would recommend to ask family members/guardians/family members first
- At one point in their life, they have been in the position you're right now, they might have even encounter a similar problem that you're in now, or at the very least - have witnessed enough in life that they can provide you a good recommendation most of the time
- It could be financial questions, or about the future -> life-changing decisions should asked for parents
- Odd cases: generational gap can make loved ones not understand what your difficulties are -> seek for friends
